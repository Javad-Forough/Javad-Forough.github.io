## Live Research Demos

### GuardNet — Graph-Attention Defense Against Jailbreak and Prompt-Leakage Attacks

GuardNet is a pre-inference graph-based defense that detects both jailbreak and prompt-leakage adversarial prompts before they reach the target LLM. It constructs hybrid token graphs integrating sequential, syntactic, and attention-derived relations, and applies Graph Neural Networks for prompt-level detection and token-level localization.

[**Launch GuardNet Demo**](https://javad-forough.github.io/GuardNet/){: .btn .btn--primary}

### DynaNoise — Adaptive Noise Injection for Membership Inference Defense

DynaNoise is an adaptive inference-time defense against membership inference attacks. It modulates injected noise based on per-query sensitivity estimated via Shannon entropy, preserving model utility while substantially reducing attack success rates.

[**Launch DynaNoise Demo**](https://javad-forough.github.io/DynaNoise-Demo/){: .btn .btn--primary}
